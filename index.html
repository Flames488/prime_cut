Our Legacy

Enugu's Most Trusted Grooming House

Prime Cut stands for excellence, culture, and modern style. Located at 1 Day Street, Enugu, we've built a reputation for delivering sharp fades, elegant braids, and premium grooming services that celebrate African hair and individuality.

Our team of certified stylists uses only top-tier tools and products to ensure every cut, shave, and style exceeds expectations. From classic gentlemen's cuts to avant-garde designs, we are Enugu's destination for confidence and class.
